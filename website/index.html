What to Expect

Everything about the way we book and check in protects your time and keeps our full attention on you.

01

Book online

Pick your service, add-ons, and barber. A card holds the spot, so you're not charged when you book. Add inspiration photos so we're ready.

02

Text & check in

We're inside Image Studios; the door stays locked to keep it calm. You'll get a check-in link 15 minutes out. Wait in your car and we'll meet you up front.

03

Sit back

A consultation first, then your service with a TV and easy music. We review it together and adjust until it's right. Pay after, by card or phone.

Cancellations: 12 hours' notice, please. No-call no-shows are charged, but you're always welcome back with good vibes and a great haircut.

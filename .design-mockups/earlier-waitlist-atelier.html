From “notify me”
to “move me up.”

Retiring the earlier-slot alert. In its place: a client hoping to get in sooner joins the waitlist for an earlier day — and you move their real appointment up in a single tap.

Was

“We’ll text you a booking link if an earlier spot opens.” The client re-books themselves, if they catch it in time.

Now

They’re on the waitlist for a sooner day. You see them there, already booked, and reschedule them the moment it opens.

# Setting up a service (Haircut-style flow) — quick guide

How a service works for the client now:

1. They tap the service.
2. **Screen 1 — the cut:** your description ("Read this first") + a required **Choose your cut** question (e.g. Standard vs Skin fade). No prices here.
3. **Screen 2 — add-ons:** optional extras (facial, hot towel) with their price + time, and a running total.
4. Then they pick a time and book.

A service uses this flow as soon as it has **a Question on it**. (No question + no add-ons → they go straight to picking a time.)

---

## How to set one up

**Settings → Services & Menu → tap a service (or "New service").**

1. **Basics** — name, price, time, color.
2. **Description** — write what the cut is, in your own words. This is the "Read this first" block. Keep it short and human. End with something like *"If that's not what you're after, tap Change and pick the one that fits."*
3. **Questions → Add a question:**
   - Question: *Choose your cut*
   - **Add an answer** for each option:
     - *Standard cut* — note "Some length kept, nothing to the skin" — **$0 · 0 min**
     - *Skin fade or specialty style* — note "Faded down close to the skin" — **+$5 · +10 min**
   - Turn **Must answer** ON (they can't continue without picking).
4. **Add-ons → Add an add-on** — paid extras (facial, hot towel) with price + time. These show on screen 2. Skip if none.
5. **Staff & pricing** — if you and Heather differ, set each barber's time/price for the service, and tap **"Set add-on prices & times"** to set per-barber price + time on each add-on.
6. **Save.**

To remove a service: open it → bottom of the screen → **Archive** (hides it, can restore) or **Delete forever** (permanent; past bookings keep their saved details).

---

## What I'd recommend

- **Skin fade is a question, not a separate service.** One clean service, your time protected by the +minutes, no menu clutter.
- **Protect time over the few dollars.** If a skin fade really adds ~15 min for you, set the answer to **+15 min**, not 10. A tight calendar costs more than $5.
- **Don't over-question it.** One required cut choice + 1–2 optional add-ons is the sweet spot. More than that feels like a form and turns people off.
- **Set per-barber times** if Heather's fade takes longer than yours, so the calendar books the right length for each of you.
- **Questions = what changes the cut** (skin fade, going shorter). **Add-ons = paid extras** (facial, hot towel). Keep them in those two buckets and it stays clean.

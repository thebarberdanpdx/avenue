Appointment text messages

How we use SMS, what you get, and how you opt in and out.

How you opt in

When you book an appointment at gotvero.com/book and enter your phone number, you opt in to receive booking confirmations and appointment reminders by text from Sanctuary Barber Co. The notice below is shown on the booking form, directly beneath the phone number field, before you confirm:

By providing your number, you agree to receive booking confirmations and reminders from Sanctuary Barber Co. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

Where it appears on the booking form

Program details

  • Messages you'll get: booking confirmations and appointment reminders
  • Frequency: varies based on the appointments you book
  • Cost: message and data rates may apply
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages
  • Questions: email contact@sanctuarybarberco.com
